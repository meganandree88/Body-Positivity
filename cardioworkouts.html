HITT Training and Cardio Workouts

I am a Division III softball player for Franklin & Marshall College who has been lifting, running, and working out since I was 12 years old. I am provided with weekly college workouts from F&M's softball speed and strength coach. The weekly workouts are a combination of HITT , cardio, and lifting workouts. I have altered these workouts to physically challenge my body while combining cardio and muscle strengthening into various workouts. These workouts burn fat while building lean muscle.

Cardio and HITT Combination Workout

This is a combination of cardio and a circuit HITT workout. This workout strengthens all important aspects of an athlete (arms, abs, and legs).

5 Round Body Weight Workout

These workouts are muscle-toning cardioworkouts. It is mainly a substitute for a rest day from cardio. These workouts were created by my softball trainer Scott Evans to tone abs, arms, and legs while increasing heart rate for maximum calorie burn. I recommend the 'Got to Love it' workout.

Leg Killer Cardio

This workout is a leg killer! It is solely cardio. I normally use this as my Saturday workout before my rest day on Sunday.
